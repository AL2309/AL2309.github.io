I used different techniques to create my photomontage on adobe photoshop. I used masking, playing around with lighting and smoothing around parts of my image. I mainly used masking to get different photos into my image that I took. It took me awhile messing around with layering to finally understand how to masking works, using inverted and the lasso tool to best cut out the photo. I also used various brighting and contrasts to make the inserted photos not look to contrasting in color. As the background is darker, with low lighting compared to the photos that I used. I found all my images in Bing, filtering it to find images that could be used and alter.
